stilling being on EST has its upsides

Oddly, if you get up nice and early, like before the sun is screaming down from the sky, bombing around in the desert is not a completely miserable endeavor. We took advantage of this fact and pointed the Jeep towards Valley of Fire State park in Overton. With the top down, a pack of donuts and Slim Jims in tow, we made quick work of the drive (fighting off urges to stop at rest area taco trucks) and managed to arrive shortly before 9am.
