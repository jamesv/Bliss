that's a wrap

No more, please.

We quit, yield, forfeit, whatever. No pool. No nap. We just need to bolt.

Don't get us wrong, this trip has been more than incredible, but there is only so much the human body is designed to withstand. Our livers, skin and brains are at capacity, so we are bowing out. With our bags are packed, the car returned and us checked out of the hotel, there is nothing more to do/eat/see. All that remains is to total it all up, summarize the important bits and plunk down on a plane for hours and wait for home to come across the horizon, welcoming us back, now as one.

We can not come close to expressing quite how much we've appreciated everyone's kindness and support, so, simply from the bottom of our hearts - thank you. We can't wait to see you all soon.
-james and amanda
