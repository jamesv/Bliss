Since our bodies are still halfway set to Michigan time, we woke up bright and early, and launched this site. The outpouring of love from everyone has been staggering, so thank you all. We celebrated this minor victory with a lovely breakfast at Mr. Lucky's, and by 10am we had reached the pinnacle of productivity of the day. At that point we hit the strip to find Amanda a new swimsuit, which was immediately put to the test with half a day's soak in the pool. Followed by a nap.

Once properly recuperated, it was decided that we would dine upon the finest buffet $10 a head could procure. We went all in with an $11 favorite on Yelp, in old vegas, and were not disappointed. After which we sauntered through Freemont street and retired to the hotel for more napping.
