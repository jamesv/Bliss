and so it begins

After lying to and deceiving folks for the past few weeks it felt great to pack out bags, kiss the dogs goodbye and head west. Travel was uneventful, James remembered how boxy Mustangs feel and we sailed through the marriage license bureau in record time.

The ceremony was just adorable we swear. Our minister was an effeminate Cheech Marin with wild gesticulation, we kissed, wished the next couple well and were out of there. We bombed around town in the convertible for a bit, grabbed some refreshments and hit the penny slots. It was just like Bridal Monthly said it would be.

By far, the scariest and most stressful part of the whole ordeal was driving through North Vegas traffic. Everything else was cake.
